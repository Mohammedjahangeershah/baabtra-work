The 16th season of the Indian Premier League (IPL 2023) ended with Chennai Super Kings (CSK) becoming champions and lifting the IPL 2023 trophy

Chennai Super Kings beat Gujarat Titans in the IPL 2023 final by five wickets in a rain-hit match at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad on Tuesday.
